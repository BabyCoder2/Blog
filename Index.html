My learning journey the past 3 years has been a roll coaster ride. One moment I think I have found a great channel on YouTube that could teach me what I need to know, only to be confused by the pleothora of others who are teaching what I am trying to learn. It doesn't help that you have those 'tech influencers' who seem to be fear mongering and making videos that make you question rather or not if this is what you truly want to do. I have wasted money on courses that are still unfinished or cancelled the subscription because I wasnt engaged in the content.

I really had to ask myself is this something that I truly wanted to do? Why do I want to do this first of all? In the beginning, it had to do everything about money. Until time has gone by that the money is a plus it's the fact that I will be doing something that I have never done before. It is an ever evolving field where I will be constantly learning. If you think about it, as we live we are forever students. Constantly evolvoing and learning just to be able to navigate life. Most jobs I hit this phase where I get bored and don't feel the least bit challenged and I am ready to leave.

The moment I came across Frontend Masters I was like ok this might actually be it. I managed to shy away from learning and still looking at countless videos on YT to only be in what they call tuturiol hell and always still on html. smh So I gave it another shot after receiving an email about the first month being a discount. So I took the dive and now I have completed the first project of the course which is this html blog. I have to say, so far Frontend Masters gives me college vibes but I love it.
